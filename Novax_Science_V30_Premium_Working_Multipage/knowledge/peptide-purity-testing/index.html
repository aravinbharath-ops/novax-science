Understanding Peptide Purity Testing

An overview of identity, chromatographic purity and why results must remain batch-specific.

Identity and purity differ

Identity testing asks whether the expected compound is present. Purity testing estimates the proportion represented by the principal detected component.

Common methods

Chromatographic methods may report purity while mass spectrometry may support molecular-identity assessment.

Results are batch-specific

Purity information should remain linked to the exact tested batch and should not be generalized to another lot.
