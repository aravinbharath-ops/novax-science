How Secure Batch Verification Works

Understand how unique codes connect a labelled product to its maintained batch record.

Unique serial matching

The verification form submits the printed serial to the secure registry. The code must match an active record exactly.

Connected record

A successful match returns product identity, strength, batch information and the certificate URL stored in the record.

Traceability, not treatment guidance

Verification supports product documentation and does not provide dosing, medical or human-use instructions.
