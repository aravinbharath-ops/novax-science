Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about the catalogue, verification process and batch documentation.

How do I verify a product?

Enter the unique serial number exactly as printed on the product on the verification page.

What appears after successful verification?

The active product record, strength, batch information, analytical result and Certificate of Analysis link where assigned.

Why can a serial fail verification?

The code may be entered incorrectly, inactive or absent from the active certificate registry.

Are the products intended for human use?

No. All Novax Science products are supplied exclusively as laboratory research materials.

Does verification replace laboratory analysis?

No. Verification supports identity and traceability but does not replace internal controls or independent analysis.

How do I submit a certificate enquiry?

Use the contact page and include the product, batch and serial details exactly as printed.
