FAQ

Research with confidence.
Verify with certainty.

Why should I authenticate every Novax Science product?+

Authentication confirms that the product corresponds to an active Novax Science production record and provides access to the associated Certificate of Analysis where available.

What information is displayed after authentication?+

Successful authentication confirms the product identity, associated batch information, analytical results and Certificate of Analysis when available.

What if my authentication code cannot be found?+

Please ensure the code has been entered exactly as printed on the product label. If the issue continues, contact Novax Science for assistance.

Are Novax Science products intended for human or veterinary use?+

No. All Novax Science products are supplied exclusively as laboratory research materials and are not intended for human or veterinary use.

How are Certificates of Analysis managed?+

Certificates are maintained within the Novax Science verification database and linked to individual production batches to support traceability and quality assurance.

How are Novax Science products tested?+

Selected batches undergo independent analytical testing by third-party laboratories. The exact testing method and reported results depend on the product and analytical service used.

Why can measured content differ from the label amount?+

Laboratory results reflect the tested sample and analytical method. Small differences can occur because of normal manufacturing variation, sample preparation and analytical measurement uncertainty.

How should research peptides be stored?+

Follow the storage instructions shown on the product label and any accompanying handling information. Correct storage is important for preserving sample integrity during laboratory research.
