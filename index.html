FAQ

Clear answers for responsible verification.

Are these products intended for human use?+

No. Novax Science products shown on this website are labelled exclusively for laboratory research and are not intended for human or veterinary use.

What happens when a code is not found?+

The result should remain unverified. A missing or incorrect code must never be presented as a successful match.

What information appears after verification?+

A verified result may display the product name, batch identifier, recorded date and a link to the maintained source record.

How are product images updated?+

Product visuals can be replaced individually in the website assets folder without changing the verification workflow.
