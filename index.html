FAQ

Clear answers for responsible research procurement.

Are Novax Science products intended for human use?

No. Products displayed here are labelled exclusively for laboratory research and are not for human or veterinary use.

How is a purity statement supported?

Any purity statement should be supported by the applicable batch certificate of analysis. Do not publish unsupported blanket claims.

Can I verify a batch online?

The page includes a verification interface ready to connect to an internal database. It must not show a successful result until real records are integrated.

Do you provide medical guidance or dosing information?

No. Novax Science does not provide treatment recommendations, dosing guidance or medical advice.
