About Novax Science

Built for transparent research-material verification.

Our objective is straightforward: present clearly identified research materials and connect supported products to their maintained batch records.

Our approach

Novax Science combines a curated catalogue with product-specific pages, secure code verification and access to analytical documentation where available.

Clear research-only positioning

Our catalogue is intended exclusively for laboratory research. We do not provide human-use, veterinary-use, medical, dosing, treatment or performance guidance.

Traceability over assumptions

Researchers should use the exact product code and batch identifier rather than relying on visual appearance or generic information. Each verified record is designed to connect the labelled format with its maintained source data.

Continuous improvement

We continue to improve product documentation, verification workflows, responsive access and educational material for researchers reviewing the catalogue.
