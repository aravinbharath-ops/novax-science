FAQ

Product and verification guidance.

Why should I authenticate every Novax Science product?+

Authentication confirms that the product corresponds to an active Novax Science production record and provides access to the associated Certificate of Analysis where available.

What information is displayed after authentication?+

Successful authentication confirms the product identity, associated batch information, analytical results and Certificate of Analysis when available.

What if my authentication code cannot be found?+

Ensure the code is entered exactly as printed. If the issue continues, contact Novax Science for assistance.

Are products intended for human or veterinary use?+

No. All products are supplied exclusively as laboratory research materials.

How are Certificates of Analysis managed?+

Certificates are maintained in the verification database and linked to individual production batches.

How are products tested?+

Selected batches undergo independent analytical testing by third-party laboratories. Methods and reported results depend on the product and analytical service.

Why can measured content differ from the label amount?+

Small differences can occur due to manufacturing variation, sample preparation and analytical measurement uncertainty.

How should research peptides be stored?+

Follow the storage instructions on the product label and accompanying handling information.
