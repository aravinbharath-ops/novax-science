Research Material Storage and Recordkeeping

A general guide to maintaining storage records, controlled access and batch traceability.

2026-07-17 · 7 MIN READ

Follow batch-specific information

Storage conditions can vary by material and format. The verified batch record and supplied documentation should take precedence over generic guidance.

Control environmental exposure

Researchers should minimise unnecessary exposure to heat, moisture and light where the product documentation identifies these as relevant factors.

Maintain a clear chain of custody

Document receipt date, responsible personnel, storage location, access, movement and disposal according to the organisation's procedures.

Separate research records

Keep product verification, analytical documentation and experimental records linked by a consistent batch identifier.
