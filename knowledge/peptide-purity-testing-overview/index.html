Peptide Purity Testing: An Overview

Explore common analytical concepts used when reviewing purity data for peptide research materials.

2026-07-17 · 7 MIN READ

Purity and identity are different

Identity testing asks whether the material corresponds to the expected compound. Purity testing estimates how much of the analysed sample is represented by the main detected component.

Common analytical methods

High-performance liquid chromatography is often used to report chromatographic purity, while mass spectrometry may support molecular-identity assessment.

Interpreting a percentage

A purity percentage should be read together with the method, chromatogram, sample preparation and reporting limitations. It should not be treated as a complete description of the material.

Use the exact batch record

Analytical results are batch-specific. Researchers should avoid applying one batch's documentation to another batch or format.
