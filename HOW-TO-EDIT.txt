NOVAX SCIENCE — EDITABLE WEBSITE V3

EASIEST METHOD
1. Open editor.html in your browser.
2. Change wording, logo path, hero image path or product details.
3. Click Download Updated content.js.
4. Upload the downloaded content.js to GitHub and replace the old version.
5. Commit. Cloudflare Pages publishes automatically.

ADDING IMAGES OR A LOGO
1. Upload the new image into your GitHub assets folder.
2. Use a simple filename such as logo.png or semaglutide-10mg.jpg.
3. Enter its path in the editor, for example:
   assets/logo.png
   assets/semaglutide-10mg.jpg
